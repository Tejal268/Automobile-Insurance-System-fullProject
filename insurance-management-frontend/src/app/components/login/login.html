What is Car Insurance?

Car insurance is a protective cover that helps vehicle owners manage expenses arising from accidents, theft, fire, or natural disasters. In India, having at least third-party car insurance is mandatory by law. It covers damage or injury caused to other people or their property. Insurance companies like Bajaj Allianz offer various plans such as third-party, own-damage, and comprehensive policies. A comprehensive policy provides wider coverage, including both personal vehicle damage and third-party liabilities. It can also include add-ons like zero depreciation, engine protection, and 24x7 roadside assistance for enhanced protection and peace of mind.
